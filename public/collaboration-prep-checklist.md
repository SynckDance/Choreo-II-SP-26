## During the Session

### Documentation Reminders
- [ ] Record audio of key discussions
- [ ] Video significant moments
- [ ] Note reactions and facial expressions
- [ ] Capture unexpected suggestions
- [ ] Write down questions that emerge

### Language Reminders
- Lead with curiosity, not direction
- "I'm imagining..." instead of "I want..."
- "What if we..." instead of "Can you..."
- Ask "What are you feeling?" before giving notes
